About

Welcome to the site!

This site was created as a creative project for IB C82, Oceans, Fall 2018. It's supposed to be a simple site that examines and summarizes the key characteristics of 7 marine phyla: Porifera, Cnidaria, Chordata, Echinodermata, Mollusca, Arthropoda, and Annelida.
