Echinoderm

Penta-radial

Characteristics

  • Penta-radial symmetry--bilaterally symmetrical
  • Tube feet and hydrostatic skeleton

Examples

  • ECHINOIDEA (sea urchins & sand dollars)
  • HOLOTHUROIDEA (sea cucumbers)
  • OPHIUROIDEA (brittle stars incl. basket stars)
  • ASTEROIDEA (sea stars)
  • CRINOIDEA (sea lilies/crinoids)
