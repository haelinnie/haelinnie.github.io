Arthropoda

Jointed Leg

Characteristics

  • Jointed appendages
  • Segmented body
  • Exoskeleton
  • Greatest number of species (over a million)
  • One of the most diverse phylum

Examples

  • CRUSTACEA (lobsters, crabs, barnacles, krill, copepods)
  • CHELICERATA (arachnids (spiders, scorpions), horseshoe crab)
  • HEXAPODA (insects)
