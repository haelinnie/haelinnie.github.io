Annelida

Segmented Worms

Characteristics

  • Bilaterally symmetrical creatures
  • Have a coelom: a fluid-filled cavity between the outer body wall and the gut.
  • Around 9000 species exist

Examples

  • Marine: Polychaeta (tube worms, bristle worms)
  • Terrestrial: Clitellata (earth worms, leeches)
  • Bobbit worm
