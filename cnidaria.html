Cnidaria

Stinging Nettle

Characteristics

  • Group defined by stinging cells called cnidoblasts on tentacles
  • Single opening for mouth/anus
  • Have radial symmetry
  • Diet: large zooplankton and small fish
  • Zooids (individual units) may take the form of: polyps or medusa

Examples

  • Anthozoans: sea anemones and corals
  • Medusazoans: jellies
    • Scyphozoa (true jellies)
    • Cubozoa (box jellies)
    • Hydrozoa (hydroids) ex. Portuguese Man of War
    • Staurozoa (stalked jellies) pretty
