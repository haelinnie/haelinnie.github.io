Porifera

Sponges

Characteristics

  • Approximately 10,000 species
  • Consists mostly of sponges
  • No true tissues (parametazoa)
  • No digestive/circulatory system
  • Suspension feeders
  • Sponges can be made out of glass, spongin and/or calcium carbonate
  • One of the oldest marine phyla we will be looking at

Examples

  • Demosponge
  • Sycon (Scypha)
  • Euspongia (Bath sponge)
